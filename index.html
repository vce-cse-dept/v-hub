Vission

To be a centre of excellence that creates synergy between academics and industry, fueling entrepreneurial spirit and instilling a research bent of mind in students, allowing them to be self-sufficient and contribute to nation development.


Mission

To provide focused support to student entrepreneurs through a supportive environment that assists them in establishing their business ideas and developing their concepts into market-ready products, as well as supporting the acquisition of business knowledge, facilitating the raising of necessary funds, and connecting them with business networks.

Why Choose V-hub?

Being Hyderabad's emerging startup incubator, V-hub provides student entrepreneurs access to technology, makers space, labs, mentors, international networks, funding and office space.

In V-hub, our vission and mission runs with three pricipals Ideate, Innovate, Incubate.

Ideate

"Student undergo transformation from identifying problems to creating solutions for the beneficiaries!"

Innovate

"Our best in class maker's space enable entrepreneurs to create innovative solutions"

Incubate

"The ecosystem V Hub created will be a ideal launchpad for entrepreneurship aspirants"

Thrust Areas

Argitech

The use of technology for farming that is developed to improve efficiency and profitability. Not only in horticulture and agriculture, agri-tech is also found in forestry, aquaculture and viticulture.

Deeptech

The Sector where any student startup has an expressed objective of providing technology solutions based on substantial scientific or engineering challenges.

Edutech

The edu tech startups has a primarily focused on ensuring that their course-offerings remain, Interactive, industry-relevant and provide adequate exposure to beneficiaries.

Fintech

Fin Tech startups represent the new technology that works towards enhancing and automating the delivery and use of financial services in various sectors.
