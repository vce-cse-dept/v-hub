Wadhwani Foundation

Collaboration with Wadhwani Foundation was been an immense support and strength for our institution. Every program organized by Wadhwani was been an Inspiration and motivation for Students.
Wadhwani NEN empowers students with knowledge and skills to create high-potential startups.

  1. College Program for students with 360-degree transformation and real-world learning
  2. Enabling knowledge and skills through Courses and Startup Labs in Colleges to create HiPOs
  3. Wadhwani curriculum with emphasis on ‘Do’ pedagogy through videos, activities and assignments, and PV pitches
  4. Practice Ventures (PVs) as the seed of entrepreneurship and job creation by fostering venture creation right at the college-level
